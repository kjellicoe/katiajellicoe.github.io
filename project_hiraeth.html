H I R A E T H Restaurant Identity

brand idenity | packaging | illustration

Created as an ode to my homeland, H I R A E T H is a hypothetical fine-dining restaurant that focuses on showcasing ingredients foraged or grown in Saskatchewan. I designed the brand identity to evoke the expansiveness of a prairie thunderstorm. To extend the brand system, I created a menu and an "at-home cocktail kit" promotional box. The logo and magpie icon were drawn by hand then digitized in Adobe Illustrator and the menu and promotional documents were created in Adobe InDesign. Photographs within the brand guide and promotional box were sourced online.
