Invasive Species Book Series

layout | typesetting | illustration | bookbinding

Invasive Species and How to Eat Them is a series of three books that highlight invasive species in Canada and provides information on how to identify them—and eat them. Content and photographs were sourced from online articles then compiled, edited, and illustrated by me. Layouts were created in Adobe InDesign, images were edited in Adobe Photoshop, and illustrations were created in Adobe Illustrator. I bound each book by hand using perfect binding and self-cover techniques.
