Optical Petrography Infographic

data visualization | animation

The Optical Properties of Minerals in CPL poster is designed to be a resource for geology students at the University of Waterloo. It communicates concepts and procedures in a simple, graphic way to help students understand the complex topic of Optical Petrography. The accompanying animation provides an introduction to the poster content, and is designed to be posted on the University of Waterloo Earth Sciences Museum's instagram account. The poster was created in Adobe InDesign with elements from Adobe Illustrator; the animation was created in Adobe AfterEffects with audio edited in Adobe Audition. Photographs for both pieces and the animation background music and Foley effects were sourced online.
