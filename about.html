Meet Katia

Katia Jellicoe found her way to Graphic Design through a Master’s in geology. Currently a student and freelance Graphic Designer, her background in the geosciences honed her strong work ethic and spurred a passion for Science Communication. Clients that Katia has worked with include the City of Cambridge and Hospice Waterloo Region.

With an eye for detail and an appreciation for a refined aesthetic, Katia produces elegant, functional design solutions.
