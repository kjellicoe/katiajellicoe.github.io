Purpose Contracting

brand identity | animation | html coding

The original concept for Purpose Contracting was not developed by me, but as a twist for this assignment we were asked to improve on someone else's design system. I loved the idea of a female-led contracting company that didn't have overly-feminine branding, and I wanted to create promotional materials that were useful enough to hang onto. The logo was created in Adobe Illustrator; the promotional materials and brand guide in Adobe InDesign; and the animated logotype in Adobe AfterEffects. The website is unpublished but fully functional and was created using a template customized with HTML coding in Adobe Dreamweaver. Photographs were sourced online.
